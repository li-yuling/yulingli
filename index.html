About

I am a Ph.D. student in Consumer and Design Sciences at Auburn University. My research focuses on AI-enhanced retail experiences, consumer trust, and consumer behavior in digital shopping environments. I am particularly interested in virtual showrooms, AI recommendation agents, personalization, and technology-enabled consumer decision making. Before joining Auburn University, I worked in fashion, retail technology, and e-commerce industries in China.
